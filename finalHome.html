The Amazing Spider-Man!

-For over 60 years, the web-slinging, wall-crawling, friendly neighborhood Spider-Man has been entertaining and inspiring millions across the world. Originally, from the great mind of Stan Lee, Spider-Man is one of the most iconic superheros worldwide. From hundreds of comics, dozens of games, and an impresisve list of blockbuster movies, Spidey has taken the world by storm and there is no sign of him stopping any time soon.

There is a lot to love about Spider-Man. In addition to his unique set of skills, Spider-Man has one of the most interesting and relatable stories to tell. He is not an alien born on a dying world, or a god, or a chosen hero with a fated destiny to fulfill. Peter Parker is a broke, uncool, unpopular, and, aside from his genius level intellect, generally unskilled. Which, especially for its time, was revolutionary. The story of Spider-Man is much darker and personal than most would give it credit for at a first glance. Spider-Man's hardest battle is not between a flying metal man, or an insane super soldier, or even living sand. Peter Parker must always choose whether he is Spider-Man or Peter Parker because he can never be both. This is a more complex topic however that deserves its own discussion. Instead, I would rather you take the time to browse this site for a crash course into the most essential elements of Spider-Man.

Yes, millions around the world love and adore Spider-Man but if you have not yet been introduced, browse this site and find out why people around the world love New Yorks favorite Web-Head.

-As you might be able to tell, Spider-Man means a lot to me, and for a very long time. At a brief glance, you may not see anything special. A dorky superhero saving the day and always doing the right thing. Another colorful character in a funny suit. But I assure you, there is much more than meets the eye with Spider-Man. His unqie set of powers, iconic moments, rich lore, and more truly set spiderman above the rest.
